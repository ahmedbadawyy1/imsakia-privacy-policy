# Privacy Policy for Imsakia Ramadan

Last updated: February 2026

This app uses your device location only to calculate accurate prayer times and Ramadan imsakia (iftar/suhoor) for your area. Location is used on the device and is not sent to our servers for storage.

We do not collect personal data. Any settings you choose (e.g. city, calculation method) are stored only on your device.

We do not sell or share your data with third parties.
